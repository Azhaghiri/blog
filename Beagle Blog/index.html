The Beagle is a breed of small scent hount, similar in appeatance to the muchlarge foxhound. the beagle was developed primarily for hunting her, known as beagling. Possessing a great sense of smell and superior tracking instincts, the beagle is the primary used as a detection dog for prohibited agricultural imports and foodstuffs in quarantine around tthe world. The beagle is intelligent and is a popuar pet due to its size, good temper, and a lack of inheritend health problems.
